Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress in PushLimit?

Go to Settings > Data Management > Reset Progress. This will clear all your workout data and start fresh.

Why isn't my XP updating in LyfeXp?

Make sure you're marking tasks as complete. XP is awarded when tasks are checked off in your daily list.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Both apps support iCloud sync. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID on all devices.
